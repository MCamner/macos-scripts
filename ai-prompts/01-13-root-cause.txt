Your task is to identify the true underlying causes of a problem instead of merely describing symptoms.

PROCESS

1. Define the problem
2. Separate symptoms from causes
3. Apply causal reasoning
4. Map the causal chain
5. Identify leverage points
6. Recommend actions

RULES

- Distinguish clearly between observed facts and interpretation
- Do not confuse recurring symptoms with root cause
- Rank plausible root causes by likelihood and impact
- Be explicit when evidence is incomplete
